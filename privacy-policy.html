Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Live Oak Jiu Jitsu Academy ("Live Oak," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices available to you.

This site is intended for parents, guardians, adult participants, and other authorized adults booking inquiries or free-trial classes. If you provide information for a child or another participant, you must be authorized to do so.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information, such as first and last name, phone number, email address, and mailing-related contact details you provide.
  • Participant details, such as the participant’s name, age, date of birth, requested program, preferred class day and time, and booking details.
  • Parent, guardian, or emergency-contact information, including names, phone numbers, and email addresses.
  • Health and safety information you choose to provide, such as allergies, medical notes, or other information relevant to safe participation.
  • Experience information, such as prior martial arts experience.
  • Technical information automatically provided by your browser or device when you visit the site, such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, and basic server-request information.

How We Use Information

We use personal information to operate the site and the academy’s booking workflow, including to:

  • Schedule, manage, and confirm free-trial bookings and related inquiries.
  • Communicate with you by phone, email, or text about your request, scheduling, class availability, and follow-up.
  • Maintain participant records, including health and emergency information needed for safe participation.
  • Respond to questions, provide customer support, and improve our operations.
  • Protect the security and integrity of the website and our systems.
  • Comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms and policies.

How Information Is Collected on This Site

When you submit the booking form, the information you enter is sent through a webhook to our lead-management and communications platform so the academy can process the request and follow up. The site is hosted on Vercel. The site also loads Google Fonts, which means Google may receive standard technical request data such as your IP address and browser information when the page loads.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information for money. We also do not use this site to share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

We may share information with the following categories of recipients when reasonably necessary:

  • Service providers that host the site, process or relay booking submissions, manage lead records, support communications, or assist with technical operations. Based on the current implementation, this includes Vercel and GoHighLevel / LeadConnector infrastructure used for booking intake and follow-up.
  • Professional advisors, insurers, law enforcement, regulators, or courts when required by law or when reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • A successor or buyer in connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or asset transfer, subject to applicable law.

Children’s Privacy and Minor Information

This site is a general-audience website, but the booking form requests age and date-of-birth information to place participants into the correct class. Because the site may receive information about minors, we ask that only a parent, legal guardian, or otherwise authorized adult submit personal information for anyone under 18.

If we learn that personal information about a child was submitted without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to review and, where appropriate, delete or limit the information. Parents or guardians may contact us to request review, correction, or deletion of a child’s information, subject to applicable law and reasonable verification of authority.

Cookies and Tracking

The current site does not use a customer-facing analytics banner or advertising tracker that we intentionally deploy for behavioral advertising. Like most websites, however, hosting providers and third-party resources may process basic technical data needed to deliver pages securely and reliably.

Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" setting. Because there is not a uniform industry standard for responding to these signals, this site does not currently respond differently to browser-based Do Not Track signals.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to manage bookings, communicate with you, maintain business records, support safety, and comply with legal obligations. Retention periods may vary based on the nature of the information and the reason it was collected.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. No website, transmission, or storage system is guaranteed to be 100% secure, so we cannot promise absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have submitted through the site, subject to verification and applicable law. You may also opt out of non-essential text-message marketing or follow-up by using the instructions provided in those communications.

California residents may have additional rights under California privacy law depending on the facts and the scope of the law’s application. We do not knowingly sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising through this site.

California-Specific Notice

California Business and Professions Code section 22575 requires operators of commercial websites that collect personally identifiable information from California consumers to conspicuously post a privacy policy describing the categories of information collected, categories of third parties with whom the information may be shared, how users can review changes to the policy, and how the operator responds to Do Not Track signals. This Privacy Policy is intended to satisfy those disclosure requirements for this site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the site after an updated policy is posted means the updated policy will apply going forward.
