REACH YOUR GOALS

Be a healthier, stronger you, in a truly empowering academy.

We have multiple programs that will keep you motivated and inspired to keep coming back for more.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

A mix of modern and old school techniques, rooted in self defense and competition tested. We offer Gi, No-Gi, Women's Only, Takedowns, Fundamentals and Open Classes.

Women's Class

Focus on standing defense and generating appropriate angles for best offense against a stronger heavier opponent. Our female practitioners have the option of also attending open classes.

Executive Program

Our instructors will identify and hone strengths while filling in gaps in technique. Private and semi-private training with IBJJF certified instructors to elevate your skills.

Youth Programs (Ages 3-17)

Sprouts (ages 3-5), Youth BJJ (ages 6-12), and Teen Classes (age 13+). All staff are Live Scanned certified to ensure your child's safety.

MEET YOUR COACHES

Learn from the best. Train with the best.

All Instructors are Live Scanned Certified

Alejandro (Alex) Lucas

Head Instructor / All Programs / Co-Owner Second Degree Black Belt

Alejandro Lucas, Second Degree Black Belt under Coral Belt Renato Magno, began Brazilian Jiu Jitsu at the age of 26. He's developed curriculum and successfully taught at all program levels including Youth, Adults, Fundamentals, Women's Program and Competition Team. Coach Alex's classes are detail oriented, focused on overall development and encouraging. His infectious enthusiasm can be seen in his teaching style. Coach Alex achieved #1 Black Belt for his age group in 2017 and 2023 for NABJJF.

Strategic, timing and systems based.

Chris Park

Instructor / Youth Programs and Adults Black Belt (May 2024)

Started at Street Sports Jiu Jitsu Academy as a white belt in 2010. 'Park,' as he's known, earned his Black Belt in May 2024 under Coral Belt Renato Magno. He became part of the Youth coaching staff as a Blue Belt and has extensive mixed class and private session experience.

Playful, creative and inventive.

Jose Meza

Co-Instructor / Co-Owner Blue Belt

Jose achieved his blue belt under Scott Singer, fourth degree Black Belt. Jose's Brazilian Jiu Jitsu focuses heavily on fundamentals and guard work.

Fundamentals-focused with strong guard work.
